Bring nature in

Direct contact with nature isn’t the only way to enjoy its well-being benefits.

Research suggests that our brains are wired to respond positively even to brief, indirect encounters with the natural world—like a photograph of a mountain or the sound of ocean waves.

This extension turns each new browser tab into a nature-inspired micro-moment that refreshes your mind and boosts your mood—right from your desk.
